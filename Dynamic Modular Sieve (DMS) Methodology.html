Alignment Formulation

For each prime module (p), the first hit point (I) after the starting point (S) is calculated with these steps:

  1. Modular Remainder (R): The position of the starting point in the module's lane: R = S mod p

  3. Initial Elimination Coordinate (I): The absolute address where the module will begin scanning: I = S + Offset
